The Best Movies According to Monisha

My top 3 tamil movies of 2023.

Leo

This action thriller stars Vijay as Parthiban, a family man living peacefully until his life is disrupted by villains, leading to intense action sequences and revelations about his true identity

Ponniyin Selvan: Part 2

Directed by Mani Ratnam, this historical epic continues the story of the Chola dynasty, focusing on political intrigue, romance, and the conflicts surrounding key characters​

Viduthalai: Part 1

A crime thriller directed by Vetrimaaran, based on the short story 'Thunaivan,' depicting the struggles of a cop involved in a conflict with a separatist group​
